FunnyDigits Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 21, 2025

FunnyDigits is a fun educational app designed to help children learn to write digits using AI and voice guidance.

Information We Collect

We collect no personal information. The app may anonymously collect images of drawn digits solely to improve the AI model.

No name, email, location, or account is required or collected.

How We Use Data

Collected data is used only to improve the accuracy of digit recognition.

Data Storage

Anonymized data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers.

Children's Privacy

The app is designed for children under 13 and fully complies with COPPA and GDPR-K. No personal data is collected.
